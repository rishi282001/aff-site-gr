RegEx


(.) - any character (.*)
(*) - 0 or more times => "a dog"
(+) - 0 or 1 time (.+)
(^) - beginning of the string or (negation [^0-7])
($) - end of the string 
([0-7]) - limit
([A-Za-z]) - limit of alphabet
.{15} - any characters within the limit
.{15,} - any characters more than the limit 
.{15,22} - any characters between the limit. 
(?=.*[a-z])  (?=.*[A-Z]) (?=.*[0-9]) (?=.*[!@#$%^&*])
